Sample Services Agreement

This sample agreement is provided for local software demos only. It is not a
real contract and should not be used as legal advice.

1. Parties. Example Client LLC hires Example Vendor Inc. to provide project
planning and documentation services.

2. Term. The agreement starts on January 1, 2026 and ends on March 31, 2026
unless both parties agree in writing to extend it.

3. Fees. Example Client LLC will pay Example Vendor Inc. a fixed demo fee of
$1,000 within 30 days after receiving an invoice.

4. Confidentiality. Each party will use reasonable care to protect non-public
business information received from the other party.

5. Termination. Either party may terminate this sample agreement with 10 days'
written notice.

6. Disclaimer. This document is fictional, simplified, and intended only for
testing the mock analysis workflow.
